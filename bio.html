Brian Gaillard - Bio

Brian Gaillard was born in East Orange, NJ and currently attends Tufts University in Medford, MA. He now uses his time to develop various programs and games. Along with programming, he enjoys the subject of marine biology, as it has been a great interest of his. He has worked on a project that created a submersible research remote controlled vehicle that can take water samples at various depths, which utilized both knowledge of programming and marine environments. Besides code and biology, he enjoys rock climbing and roller skating.

He is currently in the second semester of his second year in college.
